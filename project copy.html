1 Sonam Bajwa

Sonampreet Bajwa, popularly known as Sonam Bajwa, is an Indian actress who predominantly works in Punjabi films. After participating in the 2012 Femina Miss India contest, Bajwa made her acting debut with the Punjabi film Best Of Luck and made her Tamil film debut with Kappal.
2 Anupama Parameswaran

Anupama Parameswaran is an Indian actress who predominantly works in Telugu, Malayalam and Tamil films. She debuted with a supporting role in the Malayalam coming-of-age film Premam, which became an industry hit.

3 Rashmika Mandanna

Rashmika Mandanna is an Indian actress who works predominantly in Telugu and Kannada films in addition to Tamil and Hindi films. She is a recipient of four SIIMA Awards and a Filmfare Award South.

4 Malaika Arora

Malaika Arora is an Indian actress, dancer, model, VJ and a television personality who appears in Hindi-language films. She made her debut as a film producer in 2008, with her former husband Arbaaz Khan, founding the company Arbaaz Khan Productions, which created the Dabangg film series.

5 Arjun Kapoor

Arjun Kapoor is an Indian actor who works in Hindi films. Born to the Surinder Kapoor family, he is the son of film producer Boney Kapoor and Mona Shourie.
